Description

We are currently looking for a Sr Infrastructure Engineer who will be responsible for the design, installation, operations and maintenance of critical systems infrastructure.

This role is also responsible for leading large scale, multi-data center infrastructure projects.

Skills

Experience developing technology strategies and architecting resilient solutions.

Proven engineering ability working across multiple technologies including: CISCO network engineering, Palo Alto firewalls, EMC storage, VMWare server virtualization and high-availability solutions, and Windows/LINUX servers.

Expert ability to plan and lead large scale infrastructure projects.

Ability to create system diagrams and documentation.

Systems troubleshooting and performance tuning skills.

Engineering team leadership skills.

Outstanding verbal and written communication skills.

Very good customer service skills.

Education and Additional Experience

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, related field or equivalent work experience required, advanced degree preferred.

5-7 years’ experience preferred in systems engineering and operations. Experience must include VMware vSphere and NSX-T, Red Hat Enterprise Linux and MS Windows servers, and/or storage and SAN technologies.

3 years of technology team leadership experience (formal or informal).

Experience developing strategic plans and successfully implementing new technology solutions. Experience in design, implementation, and support of multi-data center, high-availability operations and infrastructure.

Experience provisioning and operating infrastructure through automation tools such as Ansible, Puppet, Python.

Experience supporting Middleware technology stack (WebLogic, Adobe AEM, Apache HTTPD, LDAP). Experience with Microsoft 365, MS SQL Server is desired.

Experience in design, implementation, migration, or operation in public cloud (AWS, Azure, VMC on AWS) is desired.
